Restow

The packing checklist that learns what you forget.

Free. No subscription. No ads. No account. Your packing lists never leave your iPhone and your own iCloud — and the AI that fills them runs on your phone.

  • ✓ Tell it your trip — AI drafts the list on-device, in seconds
  • ✓ Pack mode with a progress ring: pack, check, go
  • ✓ It learns what you always forget and suggests it next time
  • ✓ Return repack so nothing gets left in the hotel room
  • ✓ Reminder the evening before you leave
  • ✓ Syncs through your own private iCloud — we can't see your data
