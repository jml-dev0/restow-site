Restow Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: JULY 16, 2026

This policy is short because Restow collects no data.

We collect nothing

Restow has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. The app never sends your data to us — there is no server to send it to. The App Store privacy label says "Data Not Collected" because that is exactly what happens.

Your lists stay in your iCloud

Your trips and packing lists are stored on your iPhone and synced through CloudKit to your own private iCloud account, so they appear on your other devices signed into the same Apple ID. This sync happens between Apple and you. The developer cannot see, access, or recover your lists.

The AI runs on your phone

Packing suggestions are generated on-device using Apple Intelligence. Your trip details are never sent to us or to any third-party AI service. On devices without Apple Intelligence, the app simply works without AI suggestions.

Questions

Email restow.support@icloud.com and a human (me, Jacob) will reply.

Changes

If Restow ever changes what it does with data — for example, adding anonymous analytics — this policy and the App Store privacy label will be updated first.
