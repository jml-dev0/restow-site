Restow Support

Found a bug, have an idea, or something is confusing? Email restow.support@icloud.com. I read and answer everything.

My lists aren't syncing between devices

Restow syncs through your own iCloud. Check that both devices are signed into the same Apple ID and that iCloud Drive is on in Settings → your name → iCloud. The Settings screen in Restow lets you verify this.

I don't see AI suggestions

AI drafting uses Apple Intelligence, which needs a supported device (iPhone 15 Pro or newer) with Apple Intelligence enabled in Settings. Restow works fully without it. You can just add items manually.

How do I delete my data?

Delete your trips in the app, or delete the app itself. Because there is no server and no account, deleting the app and its iCloud data removes everything.
